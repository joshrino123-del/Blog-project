Dr. Amara Osei-Bonsu

Associate Professor · MIT Earth Systems Lab

Growing up near the Volta River in Ghana, Amara watched the water levels shift year by year. Today, her lab at MIT uses satellite telemetry and machine learning to map glacial melt in real time — work that is rewriting global freshwater forecasts and placing her at the center of the most urgent conversation of our era.
